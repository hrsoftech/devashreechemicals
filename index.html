About Us

Devashree Chemicals Pvt. Ltd.

is a growing chemical manufacturing company based in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh. Established with a vision to contribute to India’s expanding industrial and chemical sector, the company is committed to delivering reliable, high-quality chemical products that meet industry standards and customer expectations. The company focuses on efficient manufacturing practices, quality control, and continuous improvement to support a wide range of industrial applications. Devashree Chemicals aims to build long-term partnerships with its clients by offering consistent products, timely supply, and responsible business practices. With a strong emphasis on safety, compliance, and sustainable growth, Devashree Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. is steadily working toward becoming a trusted name in the chemical manufacturing industry, serving both regional and national markets.
